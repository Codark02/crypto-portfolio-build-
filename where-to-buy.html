How to Buy Crypto in 2026

Simple step-by-step guide for beginners in Nigeria — including our built-in Swap feature.

5 Simple Steps to Buy Crypto

01

Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button on our Swap page. This allows you to receive coins directly into your wallet.

02

Choose Our Built-in Swap (Easiest)

Go to the Swap page, select the coin you want to buy, enter amount, and confirm. It's instant and stays on our site.

03

Use Trusted External Platforms

If you prefer, use Binance P2P, Bybit, or OKX. Deposit Naira via bank transfer or P2P and buy your coins.

04

Complete the Transaction

Double-check the amount and fees. Confirm the swap or purchase. Your coins will appear in your wallet shortly.

05

Secure Your Crypto

Never leave large amounts on exchanges. Move your coins to a secure wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.).

✅ Recommended for Beginners: Use our built-in Swap feature. It's simple, fast, and you don't need to leave this website. Your purchased coins go directly to your connected wallet.

Important Safety Tips

🔐 Enable 2FA

Always turn on Two-Factor Authentication on your wallet and exchange accounts.

💰 Start Small

Begin with small amounts until you are comfortable with the process.

🚫 Never Share Keys

Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone — not even support.

🔍 Do Your Own Research

Understand the coin you're buying. Check its use case and market reputation.
